WHY RAMUNCHO

In 2006 I was working as an intern in a two-Michelin star restaurant in Paris. It was my first job. I was 17. I could barely speak French and was (like any respectable intern) under-qualified for the job.

The sum of the above earned me the nickname “Ramuncho”, after a character from French TV Le Bigdil. Ramuncho - the TV character - was an “odd gentleman, pretty much a fool and a stutterer, yet quite gifted for puns”.

Calling me Ramuncho, was meant an insult.





WORK I AM PROUD OF

bella puppa [✞] was China’s first edible insect snack brand. I developed the product and was a shareholder for five minutes. Bella Puppa has since acquired and it is now commercialized as Chanbala.

cvi (civic intelligence) is a data company I co-founded with the mission to democratize the access to location-data for small food service operators.

mapo tofu slider is a product I designed for White Castle entry to China. The slider featured on Vice’s Munchies as the “Best Fast Food Burger Ever”.

approximatelyfood is design project and fictional venture that challenges the current definitions of "real-food"

jiaxi [✞] was China’s version of Blue Apron. I was the cofounder and CPO. We made it to a series A.

playfulfoods is an exploration on how might we use play as vehicle to encourage kids to be more curious about food.
